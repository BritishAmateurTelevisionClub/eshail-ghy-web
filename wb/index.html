Qatar OSCAR-100 Wideband Spectrum Monitor

This wideband spectrum monitor, hosted at Goonhilly Earth Station in Cornwall, shows the spectrum of the Qatar OSCAR-100 wideband transponder onboard the Es'hail-2 satellite.

For more details on Qatar OSCAR-100 see https://amsat-dl.org/eshail-2-amsat-phase-4-a

The QO-100 narrowband websdr can be found here https://eshail.batc.org.uk/nb/
